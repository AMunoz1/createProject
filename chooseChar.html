Choice 1

You are a young child living in a poor territory of late 19th-Century England. Your parents died soon after the birth of your sister, during a barbarian raid. You ran away from the cruel caretakers at the orphanage, and now live on the streets, pickpocketing and scavenging for basic necessities. One day you hear news of a traveling trader coming through the town. The town doesn't get visitors like this often, so it is likely there will be a large crowd of curious onlookers. You are confident that you will be able to steal amidst the crowd, so you make your way over with your sister.
